SHOP.

Timeless Trinkets has a large selection of antiques, both donated and purchased, for you to choose from. While we specialize in small knick-knacks, we offer plenty of other items. Clocks, radios, typewriters, furniture...you name it! Feel free to shoot us an email if you have a specific item in mind.

LEARN.

We want to teach YOU about all things antique! Once a month, we invite a guest speaker into the store to educate our customers. These speakers range from individuals with a passion and extreme knowledge of antiques to those who are eager to teach others about the importance of reusing and restoring items to protect the environment.

CRAFT.

Our storefront also hosts various workshops and classes for customers to engage in fun activities such picture frame restoration and furniture refinishing. Timeless Trinkets believes in a high-quality, low-cost mantra meaning none of our events will ever exceed $10. We can't wait for you to join us.
